Introduction

Imagining Physics is a place for visual explanations of ideas that are easier to understand once they can be seen in motion. The projects here explore topics such as relativity, quantum mechanics, waves, geometry, and simulation through videos, browser demos, shaders, and small games.

I'm a mathematics teacher and a physics enthusiast with a passion for visualizing complex concepts in an intuitive way. I started this project to share my love for physics and to help others see the beauty and wonder of the physical world through engaging visualizations.
