Hi! I'm Anssi, a Licenciate of Philosophy, a mathematics teacher, and physics enthusiast.
Welcome to Imagining Physics.

Introduction

Imagining Physics is a place for visual explanations of advanced university-level ideas. The projects here explore topics such as relativity, quantum mechanics, electrodynamics, differential geometry. As a medium I use simulations, videos, browser demos, shaders, and small games.

I have a passion for visualizing complex concepts in an intuitive way. As much as I enjoy the abstract beauty of equations, I often feel the ideas could be presented in a more engaging and accessible way. Often one does not even need to understand the equations to grasp the big picture.

I tought myself 3D graphics and game development and started this project to share my love for math and physics and to help others see their wonder through engaging visualizations.
